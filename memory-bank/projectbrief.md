# OpenEnroll Project Brief

## Project Overview
OpenEnroll is a comprehensive enrollment and management platform for insurance agencies, tenants (businesses/organizations), and their members. The platform facilitates the management of insurance products, enrollments, commissions, and reporting across multiple user roles.

## Core Requirements

### Multi-tenant Architecture
- Support for insurance agencies, tenant organizations, and members
- Role-based access control system
- Isolated data between tenants

### Agent Portal
- Dashboard with sales metrics and activities
- Member and group management
- Product management and sales pipeline
- Commission tracking and reporting
- Account settings and profile management

### Tenant Admin Portal
- Group management with billing, contributions, and enrollment capabilities
- Agent management
- Member management
- Product marketplace
- Commission management
- Accounting and reporting

### System Administration
- Tenant management
- System-wide settings
- Marketplace administration
- Advanced configuration

## Technical Goals
- Modern React frontend with Tailwind CSS
- Node.js backend
- RESTful API architecture
- Responsive design for all user interfaces
- Scalable database design

## Business Goals
- Streamline insurance enrollment processes
- Improve commission tracking and payment accuracy
- Provide transparency in insurance management
- Reduce administrative overhead
- Enable data-driven decision making through comprehensive reporting
