## Part 3 — Summary of gaps for the care team to verify

### A. Missing from EVERY branch of Claude's Form (12 items)

These exist on the Unshared Amount form and appear on **no** path of Claude's Form:

1. **UA instructions / eligibility "I agree to the terms" agreement** *(required on UA form)*
2. **Sharing request type** — Misc / **Maternity** / Procedure / Emergency *(required)*. Note:
   the pre-screening replaces this conceptually, but **there is no Maternity path** in Claude's
   Form, and the chosen path is not stored as an answer field.
3. **When did symptoms or care for this issue begin?** *(required)*
4. **Is this a new condition?** (Yes/No) *(required)*
5. **"A new condition means…" notice** — explains pre-existing-condition and 90-day
   ineligibility rules *(eligibility-critical informational text)*
6. **Bills will exceed your $1,500 / $3,000 / $6,000 UA tier** *(required)*
7. **Do you have other insurance?** (Health / Auto / Medicaid / Workers' comp)
8. **Primary care provider name**
9. **Primary care provider phone number**
10. **Primary care provider fax number**
11. **Upload PCP office notes** (file)
12. **PCP records section heading/explainer**

### B. Missing from SOME branches only

- **"Describe in detail what happened"** *(required on UA form)* — missing from **I'm Having
  Surgery** and **I Had Surgery**. The ER branch has a narrower equivalent ("Why did you go?
  What was the diagnosis?"); "Something Else" has the free-text box.
- **"Additional information you would like to share"** — missing from **I'm Having Surgery**,
  **I Had Surgery**, and **I Had an ER Visit**.
- **Provider information** — branches A/B/C replace it with structured provider fields
  (surgeon/hospital name, NPI, Tax ID), but **"It's Something Else" has no provider field at all**.
- **Date of Service** and **document upload** — covered per-branch on A/B/C, but **"It's
  Something Else" collects no date and no file uploads**.

### C. Present on Claude's Form, but NOT on the "It's Something Else" branch

The "It's Something Else" answer skips the closing pages, so these UA-form fields are absent
from that branch:

- **PHI authorization agreement** *(required on UA form)*
- **Signer's full name** *(required)*
- **Signature** *(required)*
- **Direct Deposit** — account holder name, bank name, account type, routing number,
  account number *(whole section)*
